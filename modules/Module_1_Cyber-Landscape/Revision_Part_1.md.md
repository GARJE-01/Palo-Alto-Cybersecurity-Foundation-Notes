## ❓ Practice Questions

### Short Answer Questions

1. Explain CIA Triad with a real-world example for each pillar
2. Define threat, vulnerability, and risk—how do they relate?
3. What is phishing vs. spear phishing?
4. Explain the NIST Framework 5-step lifecycle
5. Why are small businesses targeted more than large enterprises?
6. What is Zero Trust? How does it differ from traditional security?
7. Explain IaaS, PaaS, and SaaS with examples
8. What are the main risks of BYOD?
9. Difference between compliance and security—give an example
10. Name 3 important cybersecurity regulations and what they protect

### Scenario-Based Questions

**Scenario 1:**

A company uses weak passwords and gets hacked. Identify: threat, vulnerability, and risk.

**Scenario 2:**

Employee shares a Google Drive document with "anyone with link." What type of SaaS exposure is this? How can it be prevented?

**Scenario 3:**

Company passes GDPR audit but suffers a data breach. Explain why compliance didn't prevent the breach.

**Scenario 4:**

Organization implements Zero Trust. What changes compared to traditional perimeter security?

**Scenario 5:**

New employee asks if they can use personal phone for work. As security team, what are your concerns?
