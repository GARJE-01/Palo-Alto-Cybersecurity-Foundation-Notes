# Topic 12: BYOD & Consumerization

## 📌 Definition

Employees using personal devices and consumer apps for work purposes.

---

## 🧠 Simple Explanation

**BYOD** = Bring Your Own Device → Employees use personal phones/laptops for work

**Consumerization** = Using personal apps (WhatsApp, Gmail, Google Drive) for business

---

## 🌍 Real-World Example

- Employee using personal phone to access office emails
- Team sharing files via personal Google Drive instead of corporate system

---

## ❓ Why It Matters

Company **loses control** over device security, updates, and data location.

---

## 🔑 Key Terms

| Term | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| **BYOD** | Employee's personal device for work |
| **Consumerization** | Consumer tech/apps used for business |
| **Shadow IT** | Unauthorized use of apps/devices |

---

## ⚖️ Comparison

| Aspect | BYOD | Corporate Device |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Control** | Low (company controls little) | High (full control) |
| **Cost** | Lower for company | Higher upfront |
| **Security** | Weak (depends on user) | Strong (managed) |
| **User Experience** | Better (familiar device) | Standardized |

---

## 💡 Exam & Interview Points

- Interview question: *"What are risks of BYOD?"*
- Key risks: No MDM, unpatched devices, personal malware, data leakage

---

## 🚀 Quick Revision

- ▪ Easy for users, hard for security teams
- ▪ Requires MDM (Mobile Device Management)
- ▪ Data governance becomes difficult
