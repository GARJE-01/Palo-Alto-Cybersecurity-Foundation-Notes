# Topic 17: SaaS Application Risks

## 🧠 Simple Explanation

In SaaS, data is **stored outside organization** — introduces risks:

- Data exposure to unauthorized users
- Lack of control over data location/handling
- Shadow IT (unauthorized app usage)
- Account compromise → full data access

---

## 🌍 Real-World Example

Employee uploads sensitive company data to personal Google Drive (even though it's prohibited).

---

## ❓ Why It Matters

**Data leakage risk is significantly higher** in SaaS environments.

---

## 🔑 Key Terms

| Term | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| **Shadow IT** | Unauthorized SaaS/app usage by employees |
| **Data Exfiltration** | Unauthorized data transfer out of org |
| **Account Takeover** | Attacker gains access to user account |

---

## 💡 Exam & Interview Points

- Interview question: *"What are SaaS risks?"*
- Key insight: Employees often use SaaS without IT approval

---

## 🚀 Quick Revision

- ▪ Data stored outside company control
- ▪ Users create most risks
- ▪ Shadow IT is hard to prevent
