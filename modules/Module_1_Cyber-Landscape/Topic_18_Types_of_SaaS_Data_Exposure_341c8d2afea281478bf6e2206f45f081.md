# Topic 18: Types of SaaS Data Exposure

## 🧠 Simple Explanation

**SaaS exposure happens through sharing:**

| Type | Description | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Accidental Share** | Shared with wrong person | Email sent to wrong recipient |
| **Promiscuous Share** | Shared publicly/widely | Google Drive link shared publicly |
| **Ghost Share** | Old access still active | Former employee retains file access |

---

## 🌍 Real-World Example

- Sharing Google Drive link with "anyone with link" option enabled
- Forgetting to remove old collaborators from document

---

## ❓ Why It Matters

**Most breaches happen due to human mistakes**, not sophisticated hacking.

---

## 💡 Exam & Interview Points

- Interview/exam question: *"Types of SaaS sharing risks?"*
- Key insight: Humans make mistakes more often than attackers break in

---

## 🚀 Quick Revision

- ▪ Sharing = biggest SaaS risk
- ▪ Humans = weakest point
- ▪ Can be prevented with training + tools
