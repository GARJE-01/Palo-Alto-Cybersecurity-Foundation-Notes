# Topic 20: Compliance vs. Security

## 📌 Definition

Two related but distinct concepts often confused in organizations.

---

## ⚖️ Comparison

| Aspect | Compliance | Security |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Definition** | Following rules/laws | Actually protecting systems |
| **Focus** | Checklist + documentation | Real protection |
| **Driver** | Legal requirement | Business protection |
| **Success Measure** | Audit pass | No breaches |
| **Example** | "We have GDPR documentation" | "Our data is encrypted and monitored" |

---

## 🌍 Real-World Example

Company passes GDPR audit but still gets hacked because they don't monitor systems.

---

## ❓ Why It Matters

**Compliance ≠ Security** — following rules doesn't guarantee actual protection.

---

## 🔑 Key Terms

| Term | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| **Compliance** | Adherence to laws/regulations |
| **Security** | Actual protection of systems/data |
| **Audit** | Third-party verification of compliance |

---

## 💡 Exam & Interview Points

- Interview question: *"Difference between compliance and security?"*
- Key insight: Compliance is necessary but not sufficient for security

---

## 🚀 Quick Revision

- ▪ Compliance = checklist mentality
- ▪ Security = real protection
- ▪ Both needed, but don't confuse them
