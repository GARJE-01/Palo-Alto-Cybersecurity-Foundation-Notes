# Topic 21: Important Regulations

## 🧠 Simple Explanation

**Major cybersecurity laws and regulations:**

| Regulation | Focus | Applies To |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **GDPR** | Data privacy and protection | EU citizens' data anywhere |
| **HIPAA** | Healthcare data protection | Healthcare orgs, health data |
| **PCI DSS** | Payment card security | Organizations handling payment cards |
| **SOC 2** | Service org controls | SaaS/cloud service providers |
| **NIST SP 800-53** | Security standards | US government contractors |

---

## 🌍 Real-World Example

- Bank must follow PCI DSS for credit card processing
- Hospital must follow HIPAA for patient data
- Tech company handling EU users must follow GDPR

---

## ❓ Why It Matters

**Legal penalties if violated** — can include fines, lawsuits, and loss of business.

---

## 🔑 Key Terms

| Term | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| **GDPR** | General Data Protection Regulation (EU privacy law) |
| **HIPAA** | Health Insurance Portability & Accountability Act |
| **PCI DSS** | Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard |

---

## 💡 Exam & Interview Points

- Interview/exam question: *"Name important cybersecurity laws"*
- Focus: What each law protects and who must comply
- GDPR = most commonly mentioned in interviews
