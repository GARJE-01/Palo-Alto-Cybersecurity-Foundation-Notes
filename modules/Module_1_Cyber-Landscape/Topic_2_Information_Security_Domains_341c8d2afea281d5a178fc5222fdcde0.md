# Topic 2: Information Security Domains

## 🧠 Simple Explanation

Cybersecurity extends beyond hacking—it's a **multi-domain discipline**:

- Cybersecurity (technical)
- Physical security
- Privacy
- Operational security
- Disaster recovery

---

## 🌍 Real-World Example

A strong password (cybersecurity) doesn't protect against a stolen laptop (physical security failure).

---

## ❓ Why It Matters

Security is **holistic** — one weak domain compromises the entire system.

---

## 🔑 Key Terms

| Term | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| **Operational Security** | Secure processes and procedures |
| **Disaster Recovery** | Plan to restore operations after attack |
| **Physical Security** | Control of physical access to assets |

---

## 💡 Exam & Interview Points

- Interview question: *"Cybersecurity is not only technical—explain."*
- Must emphasize multi-layered approach

---

## 🚀 Quick Revision

- ▪ Security = multi-layer (physical + digital + people)
- ▪ All domains must be equally strong
- ▪ One weak point = full system compromise
