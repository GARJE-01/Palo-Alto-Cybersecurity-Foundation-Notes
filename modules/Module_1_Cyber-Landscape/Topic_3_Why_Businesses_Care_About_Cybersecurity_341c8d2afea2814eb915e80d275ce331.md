# Topic 3: Why Businesses Care About Cybersecurity

## 🧠 Simple Explanation

**Common (wrong) assumptions:**

- "We're not a target"
- "Security is too expensive"

**Reality:** Small businesses are **easy targets** with weak security.

---

## 🌍 Real-World Example

Hackers target small shops, startups, and students because:

- Limited security infrastructure
- Minimal awareness
- Lack of resources

---

## 💡 Exam & Interview Points

- Interview question: *"Why are small businesses targeted more?"*
- **Stat: 43% of attacks target small businesses**
- Many shut down permanently after a breach

---

## 🚀 Quick Revision

- ▪ Small business = easy target
- ▪ Lack of awareness = high risk
- ▪ Cost of breach >> cost of prevention
