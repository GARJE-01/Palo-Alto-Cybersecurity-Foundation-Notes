# Topic 4: Cost of Cyber Attacks

## 🧠 Simple Explanation

Cyberattacks cause:

- Financial loss (direct + recovery costs)
- Reputation damage
- Business shutdown/closure

---

## 🌍 Real-World Example

- Data breach → Lost customers and trust
- Website downtime → No sales/revenue
- Recovery costs → Exceed initial loss

---

## 💡 Exam & Interview Points

- **Stat: 60% of businesses struggle significantly after a breach**
- Long-term damage often exceeds immediate financial loss

---

## 🚀 Quick Revision

- ▪ Money loss + reputation damage
- ▪ Long-term business impact
- ▪ Prevention is cheaper than recovery
