# Topic 7: What Attackers Want

## 🧠 Simple Explanation

**Attacker motivations:**

- Money (direct theft, ransom)
- Personal data (PII - identity theft, resale)
- System control (botnets, infrastructure)
- Business disruption (competitive advantage, revenge)

---

## 🌍 Real-World Example

- Bank hack → Direct financial theft
- Instagram hack → PII data (emails, phone numbers)
- Hospital ransomware → Business disruption + ransom demand

---

## 💡 Exam & Interview Points

- Understanding attacker motivation is key to building defenses
- Interview question: *"What is attacker motivation?"*

---

## 🔑 Key Terms

| Term | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| **PII** | Personal Identifiable Information (SSN, DOB, address) |
| **Ransomware** | Malware demanding payment to restore access |
