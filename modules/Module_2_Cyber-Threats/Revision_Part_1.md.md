## 🗺️ Attack Lifecycle Quick Map

```
🔎 Recon (spy on target)
    ↓
🛠️ Weaponization (build attack)
    ↓
📩 Delivery (send attack)
    ↓
💥 Exploitation (trigger attack)
    ↓
📥 Installation (install malware)
    ↓
🎮 C2 (gain control)
    ↓
🎯 Actions on Objectives (achieve goal)
```

**Critical**: Breaking ANY ONE stage stops the entire attack.

## 💡 Key Concepts to Remember

✅ **Attackers are not all the same** — They differ by skill, resources, and motivation

✅ **Motivation determines target** — Know why, predict what they'll attack

✅ **Modern attacks are patient** — They stay hidden and move slowly

✅ **Attacks follow a process** — 7 stages, must be sequential

✅ **Stop any one stage** — Prevents entire attack

✅ **MITRE standardizes analysis** — Makes threat intelligence comparable

✅ **Real attacks teach lessons** — Basics matter more than complexity

✅ **Most breaches preventable** — With proper security controls
