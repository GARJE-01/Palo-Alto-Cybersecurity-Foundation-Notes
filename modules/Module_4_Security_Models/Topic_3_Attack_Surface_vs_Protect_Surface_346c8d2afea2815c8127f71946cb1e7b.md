# Topic 3: Attack Surface vs Protect Surface

> Understanding What to Defend and How to Prioritize
> 

---

## 📖 SIMPLE EXPLANATION

### 🔴 ATTACK SURFACE

**All possible entry points for attackers**

- Entire network
- All devices
- All applications
- All users
- All services

👉 **Very large and growing**

### 🟢 PROTECT SURFACE

**Most critical assets to protect**

- Sensitive data
- Important applications
- Critical systems
- Key services

👉 **Small but vital**

---

## 🌍 REAL-WORLD ANALOGY

### Bank Security 🏦

**Attack Surface:** Entire city

**Protect Surface:** Vault inside

👉 You don't protect the whole city

👉 You protect the vault

---

## 🧠 CORE IDEA

### Traditional Security:

❌ Try to protect EVERYTHING

### Zero Trust Approach:

✅ Protect only CRITICAL ASSETS
