## 🎯 KEY QUESTIONS

### Q1: AI Definition

**What is Artificial Intelligence?**

✅ **Answer:** Systems that simulate human intelligence through learning, reasoning, and problem-solving

### Q2: AI vs Traditional Security

**Why is AI better than traditional security?**

✅ **Answer:** Traditional is rule-based and slow; AI detects unknown threats faster

### Q3: ML vs GenAI

**Difference between ML and GenAI?**

✅ **Answer:** ML learns from data to predict; GenAI creates new content

### Q4: Supervised vs Unsupervised

**What's the difference?**

✅ **Answer:** Supervised needs labeled data; unsupervised finds patterns on its own

### Q5: Why Deep Learning?

**Why use deep learning over traditional ML?**

✅ **Answer:** Handles larger data, higher accuracy, automatic feature extraction
