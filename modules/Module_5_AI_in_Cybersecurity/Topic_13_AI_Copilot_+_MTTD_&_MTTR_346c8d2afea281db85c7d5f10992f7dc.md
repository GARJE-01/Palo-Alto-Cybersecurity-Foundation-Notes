## ⚠️ IMPORTANT METRICS

### 💪 1. MTTD (Mean Time To Detect)

**Definition:** Time taken to detect an attack

Example:

- Threat occurs: 2:00 PM
- Threat detected: 2:15 PM
- **MTTD = 15 minutes**

✔ **Lower = better** (faster detection)

### 💪 2. MTTR (Mean Time To Respond)

**Definition:** Time taken to respond and remediate

Example:

- Threat detected: 2:15 PM
- Threat contained: 2:45 PM
- **MTTR = 30 minutes**

✔ **Lower = better** (faster response)

📌 **AI helps reduce MTTD and MTTR**
