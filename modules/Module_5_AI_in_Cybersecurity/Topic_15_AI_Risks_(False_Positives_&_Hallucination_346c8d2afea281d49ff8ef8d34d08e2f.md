# Topic 15: AI Risks (False Positives & Hallucinations)

> AI is powerful, but not perfect: limitations and dangers
> 

---

## 📖 SIMPLE EXPLANATION

**AI Risks** = Limitations and dangers of AI systems

AI can:

- Make mistakes
- Give wrong outputs
- Misidentify threats

📌 **AI systems can produce false positives and hallucinations**

---

## 🔴 1. FALSE POSITIVES

### 📖 SIMPLE EXPLANATION

**False Positive** = AI flags something as a threat, but it's actually safe

### 🌍 EXAMPLE

Normal user login → AI thinks it's an attack ❌

### 🎯 WHY IT MATTERS

❌ Wastes time

❌ Overloads security team

❌ Reduces trust in system

❌ Causes alert fatigue

---

## 🔵 2. FALSE NEGATIVES

### 📖 SIMPLE EXPLANATION

**False Negative** = AI misses a real attack

### 🌍 EXAMPLE

Malware present → AI fails to detect ❌

### 🎯 WHY IT MATTERS

❌ Very dangerous

❌ Leads to real breaches

❌ Data stolen

❌ Loss of trust

---

## 🟡 3. AI HALLUCINATIONS

### 📖 SIMPLE EXPLANATION

**AI Hallucinations** = AI generates:

- Incorrect outputs
- Misleading information
- Illogical responses

📌 **AI hallucinations = false or misleading responses**

### 🌍 EXAMPLES

- AI gives wrong security advice
- AI generates fake explanation
- AI creates fabricated data

### 🎯 WHY IT MATTERS

❌ Analysts get bad guidance

❌ Wrong decisions are made

❌ Damage can occur

---

## 🟢 4. DATA BIAS

### 📖 SIMPLE EXPLANATION

**Data Bias** = AI depends on training data

If data is biased:

- Output will also be biased
- Unfair decisions
- False patterns

### 🌍 EXAMPLE

Training data has biased examples

→ AI makes biased predictions

---

## 🔴 WHY THESE RISKS EXIST

AI depends on:

- Data quality
- Algorithms
- Training process

**AI is not fully intelligent!**

---

## ✅ SOLUTION (VERY IMPORTANT)

### ✔ HUMAN OVERSIGHT

Always verify AI output

### ✔ CONTINUOUS TRAINING

Update models regularly

### ✔ HYBRID APPROACH

AI + Human together

📌 **Human supervision is essential for safe AI use**

---

## 📊 RISK MATRIX

| Risk | Type | Severity | Solution |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| False Positive | Accuracy | Medium | Tuning |
| False Negative | Accuracy | **High** | **Human review** |
| Hallucination | Output | Medium | Verification |
| Data Bias | Training | High | Better data |

---

## ⚠️ EXAM TRAP

**AI:**

- ✔ Fast & powerful
- ❌ NOT 100% accurate
- ❌ Can make mistakes
- ❌ Needs human oversight
