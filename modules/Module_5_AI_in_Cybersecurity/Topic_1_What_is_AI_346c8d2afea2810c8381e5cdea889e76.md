# Topic 1: What is AI?

> Artificial Intelligence = Systems that simulate human intelligence
> 

---

## 📖 SIMPLE EXPLANATION

**Artificial Intelligence (AI)** = systems that simulate human intelligence

Includes:

- Learning
- Reasoning
- Problem-solving
- Language understanding

📌 **From your notes:** AI mimics human intelligence functions like learning and reasoning

---

## 🧠 HOW AI WORKS

AI uses:

- Data
- Algorithms
- Computation

To:

- Learn patterns
- Make decisions
- Solve problems

---

## 🌍 REAL-WORLD EXAMPLES

✔ ChatGPT (language understanding)

✔ Facial recognition (pattern detection)

✔ Email spam filters (classification)

✔ Recommendation systems (learning)

---

## 🎯 WHY IT MATTERS

✔ Automates complex tasks

✔ Processes large data

✔ Improves efficiency

✔ Enables new capabilities
