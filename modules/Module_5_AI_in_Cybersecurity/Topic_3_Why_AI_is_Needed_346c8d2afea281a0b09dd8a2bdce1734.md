# Topic 3: Why AI is Needed?

> Problem: Traditional security is slow and limited
> 

> Solution: AI detects unknown threats
> 

---

## 🚨 THE PROBLEM

### Traditional Security:

- ❌ Slow (manual detection)
- ❌ Rule-based (only knows about known threats)
- ❌ Cannot detect new attacks (zero-day exploits)
- ❌ Depends on human analysts
- ❌ Easily overwhelmed by volume

---

## 💡 THE AI SOLUTION

AI:

- ✔ **Detects unknown threats** (never seen before)
- ✔ **Works faster than humans** (milliseconds)
- ✔ **Handles large data** (millions of events)
- ✔ **Finds patterns** humans miss
- ✔ **Never gets tired** (24/7 operation)

📌 **AI can detect advanced attacks and reduce false positives**

---

## ⚙️ HOW AI SOLVES SECURITY PROBLEMS

1. **Traditional:** "Alert: activity does not match known rules"
    - ❌ Misses new attacks
2. **AI:** "Alert: activity is statistically abnormal"
    - ✔ Catches unknown attacks

---

## 📊 REAL-WORLD IMPACT

✔ **Malware detection:** AI catches variants humans miss

✔ **Anomaly detection:** AI finds unusual patterns

✔ **Threat prediction:** AI warns before attacks happen

✔ **Response automation:** AI blocks threats instantly
