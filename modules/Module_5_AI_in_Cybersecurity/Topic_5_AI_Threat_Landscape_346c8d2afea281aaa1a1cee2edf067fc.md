# Topic 5: AI Threat Landscape

> How attackers use AI to perform advanced cyberattacks
> 

---

## 📖 SIMPLE EXPLANATION

**AI Threat Landscape** = How attackers use AI to perform advanced cyberattacks

📌 **Cybercriminals are increasingly using AI to enhance attacks**

---

## 🚨 WHY THIS IS DANGEROUS

### Before AI:

- Attacks required skills
- Complex to set up
- Limited scale

### After AI:

- 📌 AI makes attacks easier
- 📌 Even beginners can launch advanced attacks
- 📌 Attacks scale automatically

📌 **AI allows low-skilled attackers to create powerful malware**

---

## 🔴 5 TYPES OF AI-BASED ATTACKS

### 🔴 1. AI-POWERED PHISHING

AI creates:

- Realistic emails
- Personalized messages
- Convincing social engineering

📌 **LLMs generate highly convincing phishing messages**

### 🔵 2. DEEPFAKES

Fake:

- Videos
- Audio
- Images

Used for:

- Fraud
- Identity attacks
- Impersonation

### 🟡 3. AI-BASED MALWARE

Malware that:

- Adapts
- Changes behavior
- Avoids detection

📌 **AI helps malware evolve and evade detection**

### 🟢 4. AUTOMATED HACKING

AI can:

- Scan vulnerabilities automatically
- Launch attacks automatically
- Test exploits at scale

### 🟣 5. BYPASSING SECURITY

AI can:

- Break CAPTCHAs
- Mimic human behavior
- Fool biometric systems
- Bypass authentication

---

## ⚠️ DOUBLE-EDGED SWORD

**AI is used by:**

- ✔ Security teams (defenders)
- ❌ Hackers (attackers)

📌 **AI benefits both defenders and attackers**

---

## ☢️ REAL-WORLD IMPACT

AI attacks are:

- Faster than before
- Smarter than before
- Harder to detect
- More scalable
