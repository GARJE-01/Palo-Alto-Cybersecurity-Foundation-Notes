# Topic 6: Generative AI (GenAI)

> AI that creates new content: text, images, code, audio
> 

---

## 📖 SIMPLE EXPLANATION

**Generative AI (GenAI)** = AI that creates new content

Can generate:

- Text
- Images
- Code
- Audio
- Video

📌 **Based on your module: LLMs generate content using massive datasets from the internet**

---

## 🧠 WHAT ARE LLMs?

**LLM (Large Language Models)** = type of AI that:

- Understands language
- Generates human-like text
- Learns from patterns

📌 **LLMs use large datasets and learning techniques to produce responses**

---

## 🌍 REAL-WORLD EXAMPLES

✔ ChatGPT

✔ AI chatbots

✔ Code generators (GitHub Copilot)

✔ Image generators (DALL-E)

✔ Video generators

---

## ⚙️ HOW GENERATIVE AI WORKS

1. **Collect huge data** from the internet
2. **Train model** on this data
3. **Learn patterns** in language/images
4. **Generate output** based on prompt

📌 **Data must be high-quality and well-structured for accurate results**

---

## 🔴 DEFENSIVE USES (GOOD SIDE)

AI helps defenders:

- Detect threats
- Analyze logs
- Automate security tasks
- Generate security reports
- Create security policies

---

## 🔵 OFFENSIVE USES (BAD SIDE)

Attackers use GenAI for:

- Phishing emails
- Malware generation
- Social engineering
- Credential stuffing
- Impersonation

📌 **AI can generate realistic phishing content easily**

---

## ⚠️ RISKS OF GENERATIVE AI

### 🔴 1. FAKE CONTENT

- Deepfakes
- Fake emails
- Misinformation

### 🟡 2. DATA LEAKAGE

- Sensitive info exposure
- Training data leakage
- Privacy violations

### 🔵 3. MISUSE BY ATTACKERS

- Automated cyberattacks
- Large-scale phishing
- Content manipulation

---

## ⚠️ LIMITATIONS

GenAI:

- Not always accurate
- Depends heavily on data
- Can produce wrong outputs
- Hallucinates sometimes

📌 **AI depends heavily on quality training data**
